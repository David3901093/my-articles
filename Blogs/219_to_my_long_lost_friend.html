to my long lost friend

2016-10-09 23:24
when i was a child,my mother taught me ABC songs.at the beginning of my trip to English world.later,when i entered the primary school,i saw a beautiful. english world in my eyes.that was the very beginning of my english study.i think it was his unique charm that made me love him.now,we are the best friend forever.
i have written a lot of chinese article,but i don't even write a word in english.therefore,i am going to say some words in my heart to my best friend and to my classmates.
maybe at the beginning,many people feel frustrated to deal with vocabulary,but i think there must be a misunderstanding between words and learners. when i study english,i try to imagine a picture of every word,so to me,every word has its own story,however interesting or boring,this method will create a pleasant atmosphere for learners.i have learned a lot of things from my old friend .the value of life,the responsibility that everyone should take,the sense of success that it bring to me form a colorful life.i believe there should be tears,. there must also be some laughter, but whoever you are,and whoever you want to be,you should remember the experience of study in an essential part of your life.so,i hope that english, my old friend,will be your friend too. not for any purpose,just for your favorite.
remember,there is a song called yesterday once more,when i listened to the radio,waiting for my favorite songs.when they played i would sing along,it made me smile.it was a kind of real emotion from my deep heart,english and i are just like long lost friends.i am always unsatisfied with my poor vocabulary for i can't speak fluent english and thus making a gap between my friend and me. after college entrance examination,we said good-bye to each other,with sorrow and nostalgia .to my surprise, we soon meet again on campus.
i think it must be godness.oh i hope to spend next four years with you together,from CET4 to TOFEL,we share tears and laughters,pain and sweet,shall we?

yours
sincerely
david Ge
