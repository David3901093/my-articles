Why do we learn English？（我们为什么要学英语）

2019-04-19 13:04
I have ever listened to a TED speech Addressed by Jay walker talking about English mania,. A new mania Is sweeping Across the world nowadays. From England to Germany, From Latin America to Brazil,, From India to China, Over 2 billion people is studying English In different ways. Then, A question has been raised: Why do English so popular all over the world And why should we learn English?

Jay Walker said that Many of us study English for opportunity. Well, This is a good answer. It describes The motivation to study English impartially. However, I don't think so. From a Chinese college student's View, I believe that many of us Study English for interest， Not for interests. That's to say, A student Studies English just for ' Gaokao', Which means college entrance test. They study for three years And spend more than 12 hours a day to read books Just for a good Score To enter a Top university. Well, That sounds A little Ironic, But it is the fact We cannot deny. The intensity of learning English It's hard to imagine, Unless you have witnessed it.

Therefore, Somebody will be confused: I'm a Chinese, Not English, So why do I learn English? Can English bring me a bright future? Due to the pressure Put on us by our teachers ,Most of us have hated English for many years And think that English is the most difficult language in the world. They study English by shouting , exclaiming,reciting and repeating. Many college students intend to spend A day To remember all the words on the book. However, When they get to the Examination room, they find that what they kept in their mind Have already gone away. This makes them Crazy. I admit that Such condition Will not be unique. It is said that China has become the biggest country Using English as a second language， But How many people can speak fluent English？ The answer may surprise you： Less than 1%. Just as a Chinese teacher said: how can you learn English If you can't Study Chinese well？ I don't agree to it more. You native language is your life. Don't catch the shadow and lose the substance.

Then Why do we learn English? I think that English is A tool, A tool for culture communication. If you want to go abroad, You must study English well, Or you will be dead due to hunger. There is no doubt that English is a world Language And improves Global communication,But that's all. The reason why English is so popular is that The British Has ever been the Emperor On which the sun never sets. Due to the Industrial revolution, The Productivity In British Rose rapidly. And their power over the world Enabled Their language to be the first one To spread away. This is the Historical influence. I have imagined that If Our Chinese could conquer the World Then can we Abandon English？ The answer will be sure,But Such things will never Happen.

There are many other languages in the world. Mathematics is the language for science, Music is the language for emotion, Philosophy is the language for our thoughts And Literature is the language For Our Soul. Yes, To me, English is a best friend, But Chinese Classic literature is my lover. The Chinese character Gives me the power to conquer Adversity, To face with sufferings, And the force to go on. The beauty of Chinese fascinates me. I'd like to Fly in the sky of Chinese literature And Look for my Bright future .Maybe we have to Learn English For some Necessary Aims. But remember If you really want to Succeed, Don't aim for it. Just do What you like And it will come naturally.

Whatever, English is not our enemy, It is just tool, An implement For us to use. It is not As significant as it seems. Don't be nervous.

（以下为百度中文翻译，英文是我自己写的，懒得再翻了）

我听过杰伊·沃克（JayWalker）发表的关于英语狂热的TED演讲。如今，一种新的狂热正在席卷全世界。从英国到德国，从拉丁美洲到巴西，从印度到中国，超过20亿人正在以不同的方式学习英语。然后，一个问题被提出：为什么英语在世界各地如此流行？为什么我们要学习英语？

杰伊·沃克说，我们很多人都是为了机会学习英语。好吧，这是一个很好的答案。它客观的描述了学习英语的动机。不过，我不这么认为。从一个中国大学生的角度来看，我相信我们很多人学习英语是为了利益，而不是为了兴趣。也就是说，一个学生学习英语只是为了“高考”，也就是说大学入学考试。他们学习了三年，每天花12个多小时看书，只是为了考上一所顶尖大学。听起来有点讽刺，但这是我们不能否认的事实。学习英语的强度很难想象，除非你亲眼目睹。

因此，有人会困惑：我是中国人，而不是英国人，那我为什么要学英语？英语能给我带来光明的未来吗？由于老师给我们施加的压力，我们大多数人多年来都讨厌英语，认为英语是世界上最难的语言。他们学习英语的方法是大喊大叫、背诵，和重复。许多大学生打算花一天时间记住书上的所有单词。然而，当他们到达考场时，他们发现他们脑海中的记住了单词都已消失得一干二净。这让他们疯狂。我承认这样的情况并不是独一无二的。据说中国已经成为使用英语作为第二语言的最大国家，但是有多少人能说流利的英语呢？答案可能会让你吃惊：不到1%。正如一位中国老师所说：如果你不能学好汉语，你怎么学英语？我再同意不过了。你的母语就是你的生命，千万不要舍本逐末。

那我们为什么要学英语？我认为英语是一种工具，一种文化交流的工具。如果你想出国，你必须学好英语，否则你会因饥饿而死。毫无疑问，英语是一门世界性的语言，它改善了全球交流，但仅此而已。英语之所以如此受欢迎，是因为英国曾经是日不落帝国。由于工业革命，英国的生产力迅速提高。他们在世界上的威慑力使他们的语言成为第一个传播出去的语言。这就是历史的影响。我曾设想，如果我们的中国人能够征服世界，那么我们能不学英语吗？答案是肯定的，但这样的事情永远不会发生。

世界上还有许多其他语言。数学是科学的语言，音乐是情感的语言，哲学是思想的语言，文学是灵魂的语言。是的，对我来说，英语是最好的朋友，但中国古典文学是我的爱人。汉字给了我战胜逆境、面对苦难的力量和继续前进的动力。汉语的美使我着迷。我想飞向中国文学的天空，寻找我的光明未来。也许，我们必须为了一些迫不得已的目的学习英语。但请记住，如果你真的想成功，不要追逐它。只要从心所欲，它就会自然而然地到来。

不管怎样，英语不是我们的敌人，它只是我们使用的工具。英语其实并不像看上去那么重要。放轻松一些。
