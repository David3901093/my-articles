A letter to my teacher

2012-10-14 08:31

Dear Teacher

   It’s over a month since I met you. You have made a good impression on me. You’re a good friend as well as a good teacher. I hope you can teach us three years. You have a way of making your class enjoyable. We’re very active on your class. We’re getting on well, don’t we ？I’m very thankful. I remembered the first day at school. I didn’t catch the school bus you phoned my dad and made me go back home safely. Thank you very much! You are our best friend forever! You are just like our big sister, care for us if we were your brothers. You’re very humorous. There are a lot of laughter in your class. I am glad to meet you, so do my classmates. What do you think of us?

                              Best wishes

                                                    Yours

                                                  David Ge

亲爱的老师

自从我遇见你已有一个多月。你已经给了我们一个极好的印象。你不仅是一个好的老师而且是一个很好的朋友。我希望你能教我们三年。你有自己的方式使你的课愉快。我们在你的课上非常活跃。我们相处得很好，不是吗？我对于你很感激。我还记得第一天上学。我没赶上校车，你爸爸打了电话，让我去安全回家。谢谢你！你是我永远的好朋友！你就像我们的大姐姐，照顾我们，就好像我们是你的小弟弟。你很幽默。在你的课上有许多的欢笑。我很高兴见到你，我的同学也是一样。你呢？？

万事如意

你的

葛兆磊
