On bike sharing services.

2017-04-13 19:32
Nowadays,Bike-sharing Services are getting more and more popular in our society, which I think is a double-sword to our college students.Nowadays,Bike-sharing Services are getting more and more popular in our society, which I think is a double-sword to our college students.In my opinion,we should be careful with Bike sharing services and obey the moral rule when we share bikes.
At first,what I want to say is that Bike sharing services take full advantage of our limited resources.As we all know,the population is getting larger and larger for the time being,which has led to a shortage of material resource.Therefore,Bike sharing services are a good method to alleviate the lack of social resources.On the other hand,we can recycle the bikes to let more people use it,which shows the idea of environmental production.
However.There is also a problem that when we use the bikes ,not all people have a high level of discipline so that they will take the bikes for some reason. It is necessary to enforce the supervision of Bike sharing services.Meanwhile, relevant departments had better carry out a plan to solve the problem.
To sum up,Bike sharing services are new ways to share the resources in our society.when we use them,it's our duty to obey the related regulations.It also requires self-discipline.only in this way can the Bike sharing services create real convenience for us.
